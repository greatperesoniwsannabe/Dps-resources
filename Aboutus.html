Redefining Student Support

Our Genesis

Launched in 2025-March by Rithvik Eravathri and Aryan Damera Foundation, DPS Student Resources emerged from a simple observation: even in privileged environments, students often struggle to access consistent academic support. Our platform bridges this gap through:

Peer-Validated Content

Resources curated and verified by top-performing students across grades

24/7 Accessibility

Round-the-clock access to study materials from any device

Discreet Support

Anonymous access to maintain student privacy
