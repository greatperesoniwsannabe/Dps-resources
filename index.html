Welcome to DPS-H Student Resources!

This website is created for students who struggle to access notes in school. There might be various reasons for missing classes, but the challenges remain the same. To address this, we've worked closely with students from each grade to curate resources that are thoughtfully designed to meet their specific needs. This is a private initiative designed to support students and is not officially affiliated with the school.


Our goal is to ensure that no student feels left behind, regardless of their circumstances. Whether you're catching up on missed lessons or looking to strengthen your understanding, you'll find what you need here. The resources are crafted with care and input from students themselves, ensuring they're relevant and practical. We believe in bridging gaps and empowering students with the tools to excel.
